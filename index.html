Elise Ngo
Webmaster
I was first introduced to coding in my early teens when I wanted to customize my Neopets (may she rest in peace) profile page.
I dedicated myself to language studies and backpacked the world while I was young and fearless. Now, equipped with a masterful ability to learn languages and sharp problem-solving skills, honed by navigating unfamiliar territories and adapting to different cultures, I am eager to transition careers and break into software engineering.
I am obsessed with what I do offline, that influences what I do online. I am passionate about reconnecting with my cultural identity through cooking, trying to read far too many books about completely disparate topics at once, investigating foundational design principles spanning various domains, being in the ocean, and exploring the beauty and strength of which my body is capable.
I once found myself in Gran Canaria, a Spanish Island off the coast of Africa, without a place to stay or a way to withdraw money. Someone whom I had interacted with earlier that day offered to help me find accomodation and told me "there are always many solutions". In the face of an obstacle, I often remind myself of this.
I speak Portuguese, Spanish, some French, and micro-Vietnamese.
